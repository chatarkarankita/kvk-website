State-of-the-art facilities for agricultural training, research, and outreach

Overview

KVK Jalgaon Jamod is equipped with modern infrastructure to support agricultural education, demonstration, and extension activities. The facilities play a key role in providing effective training and services to farmers and stakeholders.

Infrastructure Facilities

Administrative Building

Equipped with office spaces, meeting rooms, and a fully furnished training hall with audio-visual aids. Reception area and display board for showcasing recent activities.

Soil and Water Testing Laboratory

The laboratory is equipped for testing soil health, water quality, and providing fertilization recommendations based on soil analysis.

Demonstration Farm

20 hectares of cultivable land used for crop demonstrations, on-farm testing, and Integrated Farming System (IFS) projects. Drip and sprinkler irrigation systems are installed.

Farmer's Hostel

A well-maintained facility offering accommodation for visiting farmers during training programs. Capacity of 20-30 farmers with dining and sanitation facilities.

Training Halls

Equipped with projectors, whiteboards, and PA systems. Seating for 50-80 participants. Used for farmer training, SAC meetings, and awareness programs.

Library and Resource Center

A dedicated space for agricultural books, journals, digital learning materials, and access to internet-based resources.

Vermicomposting & Azolla Units

These units demonstrate organic farming practices such as vermicomposting and Azolla cultivation, promoting bio-resource management.

Farm Machinery & Tools

Includes mini tractors, seed drills, sprayers, and irrigation kits used for training and rented to farmers for their fields.

Conference Room / Meeting Hall

A space used for official meetings, video conferencing, and Scientific Advisory Committee (SAC) sessions.
