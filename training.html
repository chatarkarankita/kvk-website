Training Programmes

Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) Jalgaon Jamod conducts a wide range of training programmes aimed at empowering farmers, rural youth, and extension personnel. These programs focus on practical skills, modern techniques, and entrepreneurship development in agriculture and allied sectors.

Training Categories

For Practicing Farmers

  • Crop production techniques
  • Integrated pest & disease management
  • Water & soil conservation
  • Horticulture practices

For Rural Youth

  • Nursery & post-harvest training
  • Vermicomposting
  • Mushroom cultivation
  • Poultry & goat farming

For Extension Personnel

  • Technology updates
  • Field workshops
  • Communication skill training

Special Features

  • OFT: On-Farm Testing for localized technologies
  • FLD: Front Line Demonstrations on farmer fields
  • ICT: Mobile-based advisories and online training
