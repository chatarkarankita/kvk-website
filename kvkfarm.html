Farm Overview

Complete overview of land utilization, infrastructure, and farm activities

Farm Location & Area

Location: Jalgaon Jamod, Buldhana District, Maharashtra

Total Area: 45 acres used for training, demonstrations, seed production, and horticulture development.

Land Utilization Overview

On-Farm Infrastructure

  • Drip & Sprinkler irrigation systems, solar pump units
  • Tractor, power tiller, seed drill, sprayers, cultivators
  • Seed and grain storage godowns, bins
  • Soil & water testing laboratory
  • Polyhouse and shade net facilities for vegetable and flower crops

Major Farm-Based Activities

  • Front Line Demonstrations (FLDs)
  • On-Farm Testing (OFTs)
  • Seed production and certified seed distribution
  • Organic and integrated farming system implementation
  • Farmer Field Schools and practical exposure visits
  • Agroforestry, soil health improvement programs
