History

Krishi Vigyan Kendra has been sanctioned to Satpuda Education Society, Jalgaon Jamod, Buldana by Indian Council of Agriculture Research, New Delhi vide letter No. 3-4/94-KVK-AEII dated 19.10.1994 for catering need based trainings to Practicing Farmers, Rural Youth and In-service Extension Functionaries, on-farm testing and Front Line Demonstration of different crops, which are grown in Buldana District.

Achievements

Training Programs:- KVK Jalgaon Jamod has conducted numerous training sessions aimed at equipping farmers, farm women, and rural youth with modern agricultural techniques, thereby improving productivity and income.

Frontline Demonstrations (FLDs):- The center has implemented FLDs on various crops and technologies, showcasing improved practices directly in farmers' fields to encourage adoption.

On-Farm Trials (OFTs):- By conducting OFTs, KVK Jalgaon Jamod assesses the performance of new technologies under local conditions, facilitating evidence-based recommendations to the farming community.

Collaborative Projects:- The KVK has partnered with various organizations and participated in government initiatives like the Jalyukta Shivar Abhiyan, contributing to water conservation and management efforts in the region.

Publications and Outreach:- Through newsletters and other publications, KVK Jalgaon Jamod disseminates valuable agricultural information, success stories, and updates on its activities to a broader audience.
