Demonstration Units

Practical Learning Models for Sustainable Agriculture

Purpose of Demonstration Units

The demonstration units serve as live models to showcase practical, sustainable, and innovative agricultural techniques. They help farmers, students, and agri-entrepreneurs learn through hands-on experience.

Major Demonstration Units

  • Vermicompost Unit: Organic composting using Eisenia fetida worms
  • Azolla Cultivation: Protein-rich livestock feed production
  • Drip & Sprinkler Irrigation: Water-saving technology demonstration
  • Soil Testing Lab: Soil health and nutrient advisory
  • Protected Cultivation: Polyhouse and shade net vegetable farming
  • Backyard Poultry: Local breed housing and management
  • Goat Rearing Unit: Scientific goat farming practices
  • Bee Keeping Unit: Apiculture and honey production
  • Nursery Unit: Sapling production and nursery techniques
  • Integrated Farming System: Model for sustainable mixed farming
